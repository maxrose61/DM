Washington, D.C.

Sarah Moore Johnson, JD, is a founding partner of Birchstone Moore LLC, a boutique law firm focusing on estate planning and estate administration. Mrs. Johnson and two of her colleagues from Venable LLP started the firm in 2014 with the goal of modernizing the estate planning practice. Mrs. Johnson is a leader in the Washington, DC estate planning community, having chaired the Estate Planning Committee of the DC Bar Taxation Section, served on the Steering Committee of the DC Bar Estates, Trust and Probate Law Section, and as a Board Member of the Washington, D.C. Estate Planning Council.  


Mrs. Johnson was a subcommittee Chair of the American Bar Association’s Task Force for Tax Reform, which helped author a report submitted to Congress in 2012, and she is a fellow of the American College of Trust and Estate Counsel. She was recognized by the American Bar Association Taxation Section as a Nolan Fellow in 2008 for her leadership in the Tax bar as a young lawyer. When not working, Mrs. Johnson travels the world with her husband to attend major sporting events and concerts, and she is an active supporter of the local food movement.
