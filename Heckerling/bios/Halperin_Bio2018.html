Alan S. Halperin is a partner and co-chair of the Personal Representation department of Paul, Weiss, Rifkind, Wharton & Garrison LLP in New York City. He also is an adjunct professor at New York University School of Law, where he has taught a number of courses involving taxation of trusts and estates, estate and gift tax and charitable giving. He is a Fellow of the American College of Trust and Estate Counsel. In addition, Mr. Halperin serves as a co-chair of the Estate and Gift Taxation Committee of the Tax Section of the New York State Bar.
