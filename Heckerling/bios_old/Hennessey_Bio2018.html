William T. Hennessey practices with the law firm of Gunster, Yoakley & Stewart, P.A. in West Palm Beach, Florida. He Chairs the firm’s probate and trust litigation practice and is a Chair of its Private Wealth Services group. He is a Fellow of the American College of Trust and Estate Counsel and the past chair of the Florida Bar Real Property Probate and Trust Law (“RPPTL”) Section’s Probate and Trust Litigation Committee and its Legislative Committee (where he was responsible for overseeing and addressing all aspects of probate, trust, and guardianship legislation). He is currently the Probate Division Director for RPPTL. Bill has been recognized as Lawyer of the Year in Trust and Estate Litigation by Best Lawyers in America, a Florida Super Lawyer, a Top Lawyer by South Florida Legal Guide, and is AV-rated by Martindale-Hubbell. He has authored numerous articles and is a frequent lecturer on probate and trust litigation topics.
